Content

Home page

Looking to find trails in the greater Reno area. You have come to the right place. Here at Running in Reno we help find the right run for you. Whether you are looking for difficult trails with lots of hills, or a beginning run in a shaded environment we can help you. Enjoy the varied landscapes and scenery the Reno area has to offer experience the great feeling as you become one with the trail. Get into your zone.

Trails

SteamBoat Ditch Trail: This trail is a wide, dusty, well-worn double track that parallels Steamboat Ditch, a seasonally-flowing irrigation ditch that carries water from the Truckee River in Verdi to farms and ranches in South Reno. Reno Truckee River Trail: A fun paved urban walk through downtown and along the Truckee River. The trail can be continued in either direction. Experience this 3.5-mile out-and-back trail near Reno, Nevada. Generally considered an easy route. This is a very popular area for birding, road biking, and running, so you'll likely encounter other people while exploring.
