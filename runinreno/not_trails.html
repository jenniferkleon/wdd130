Hidden Valley County Park Trail

Explore this 4.2-mile loop trail near Reno, Nevada. Generally considered a challenging route, it takes an average of 2 h 30 min to complete. This is a very popular area for birding, hiking, and mountain biking, so you'll likely encounter other people while exploring. The best times to visit this trail are March through November. Dogs are welcome and may be off-leash in some areas.

Sparks Marina

As two cities that share the Truckee Meadows, the cities of Sparks and Reno have grown together over the past several decades forming one metro area.The Sparks Marina, which has become a go-to place in Sparks for outdoor recreation is right in the heart of the metro area. Get some fresh air with the locals with stand-up paddleboard and kayak rentals, stretch your legs along the two-mile walking and biking trail that surrounds The Marina. This is a paved course that is well lite at night.

Reno Truckee River Trail

A fun paved urban walk through downtown and along the Truckee River. The trail can be continued in either direction. Experience this 3.5-mile out-and-back trail near Reno, Nevada. Generally considered an easy route. This is a very popular area for birding, road biking, and running, so you'll likely encounter other people while exploring.
